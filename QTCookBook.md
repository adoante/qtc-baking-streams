## **Clam Chowder**

**Ingredients**  
2 Can Clams  
2 Cup Potatoes  
1 Cup Celery  
1 Cup Onions  
½ Sq. Butter  
¾ Cup Flour  
½ Tsp Sugar  
1 Qt Half And Half

**Directions**  
1\. Simmer together clams, potatoes, celery and onions in the clam juice  
2\. Melt butter and blend in flour for thickness  
3\. Add a dash of sugar  
4\. Salt and pepper to taste  
5\. Add cream, other ingredients and clams

## **Whipped Cream Cake**

**Ingredients:**  
1 cup butter, softened  
3 cups sugar  
6 eggs  
3 cups cake flour, sifted  
1 cup heavy whipping cream  
1 teaspoon vanilla extract  
1 teaspoon lemon extract  
Fresh berries or fruit to garnish  
Confectioners sugar

**Recipe (16 servings):**  
Heat oven to 325 degrees.  
Cream the butter and sugar in a mixing bowl until light and fluffy.  
Add the eggs, flour and whipping cream and mix well.  
Stir in flavourings.  
Spoon into a greased and floured bundt pan.  
Bake for 1½ hours or until it tests done.  
Cool in pan for several minutes.  
Invert onto wire rack and cool completely.  
Garnish with confectioners sugar and fresh berries.
