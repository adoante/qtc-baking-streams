## **Fucking Muffins** {#fucking-muffins}

*This is easy easy anyone can make this.* 

**Equipment**  
You need a mixing bowl, a muffin pan, cupcake liners and an oven.

**Ingredients**  
½ Cup Butter   
1 ¼ Cups Sugar   
2 Eggs   
1 Tsp Vanilla Extract   
2 Cups Flour (And Some Extra)   
½ Tsp Salt   
2 Tsp Baking Powder  
 ½ Cup Milk   
2 Cups Blueberries   
½ Cup Turbinado Sugar (brown sugar will work if you don’t want to buy turbinado)
