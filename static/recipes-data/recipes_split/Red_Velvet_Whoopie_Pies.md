## **Red Velvet Whoopie Pies** {#red-velvet-whoopie-pies}

*Difficulty Rating: ⭐️⭐️* 

**COOKIE**  
3 Cups All-Purpose Flour   
6 Tbsp Unsweetened Cocoa Powder   
½ Tsp Salt   
1 ½ Tsp Baking Soda  
½ Cup Salted Butter Softened   
1 Cup Light Brown Sugar Packed   
⅓ Cup Granulated Sugar   
2 Large Eggs   
2 Tsp Vanilla Extract   
1 Tbsp Red Liquid Food Coloring or Red Gel Food Coloring   
1 Cup Buttermilk 

**Cream Cheese Filling**:   
½ Cup Salted Butter Softened   
8 Oz Packages Cream Cheese Softened   
3 Cups Powdered Sugar   
1 Tsp Vanilla Extract   
2 \- 3 Tbsp Heavy Cream Or Milk 

You NEED a cookie sheet for this as well as parchment paper (or something to line the pan so it does not stick. 

OPTIONAL but I am making mine heart shaped, so if you want them heart shaped you need a piping bag or a gallon ziplock bag. 

Need mixing bowls and an oven. 

Frosting aka the filling is always easier to do with a mixer but you can do it by hand. 

ALSO Optional this is a VDAY style treat so if you wanna get some cute sprinkles for it you go glen coco 

**\*TIP** Leave your butter and cream cheese out on your counter the night before
