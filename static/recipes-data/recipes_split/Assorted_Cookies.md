## **Assorted Cookies** {#assorted-cookies}

*“They are VERY VERY easy and anyone can make it. All you need is an oven, bowls, and baking pan or sheet to bake them on. If you haven't tried either, they are two of my favorites and I encourage you to give it a go\!” \- QTCinderella, 2020*

**Peanut Butter Cyclops Cookies**  
1 ¾ Cups All-Purpose Flour  
1 Tsp Baking Soda  
½ Tsp Salt  
1 Cup Sugar  
½ Cup Dark Brown Sugar (Packed)  
8 Tbsp (1 Stick) Unsalted Butter, Cut Into Small Pieces  
½ Cup Smooth Or Chunky Peanut Butter (Not Natural)  
1 Large Egg  
1 Tsp Pure Vanilla Or Almond Extract  
30 Chocolate Kisses, Unwrapped

**Snickerdoodles**  
1 Cup Unsalted Butter (Softened)  
1 ½ Cups Sugar  
2 Large Eggs  
2 Tsp Vanilla  
2 ¾ Cup Flour  
1 ½ Tsp Cream Of Tartar  
½ Tsp Baking Soda  
1 Tsp Salt  
¼ Cup Sugar  
1 ½ Tbsp Cinnamon
