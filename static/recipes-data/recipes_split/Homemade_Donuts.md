## **Homemade Donuts** {#homemade-donuts}

**Ingredients**  
Water   
Yeast   
Flour   
Salt   
Dry Milk Powder (this might be hard to find but all stores have it esp rich people stores like whole foods and sprouts)   
Sugar   
Butter   
16 Oz Veggie Oil 

**Topping Options:**  
\- Extra Sugar (for Sugar Coated Donuts)  
\- Powdered Sugar (Powdered Sugar Coated Donuts)  
\- Ground Cinnamon And Sugar (Cinnamon Covered Donuts)   
\- Powdered Sugar & Milk (Glaze)   
\- Powdered Sugar & Milk & Maple Extract (Maple Glaze)   
\- Powdered Sugar & Milk & Cocoa Powder (Chocolate Glaze)
