## **Churros and Dipping Sauce** {#churros-and-dipping-sauce}

*Difficulty:⭐️*   
*Time: \~1 hour* 

**Equipment**  
\- Large Pot or Deep Skillet (Anything to fry the churros in)   
\- Hand/Stand Mixer (Alt: Mix by hand)  
\- Saucepan   
\- Mixing Bowls   
\- Metal Tongs [https://amzn.to/3tjUAUW](https://amzn.to/3tjUAUW) (Alt: long chopsticks [https://amzn.to/3zj3A0s](https://amzn.to/3zj3A0s) or metal slotted spoon)   
\- 16” Piping bag fitted with a rounded star tip (no bigger than ½”) (Alt: ziploc bag)

**Churros**   
1 Cup (250ml) Water   
¼ Cup (56g) Unsalted Butter, Diced Into Small Cubes   
1 Tbsp (13g) Granulated Sugar   
¼ Tsp Salt   
1 Cup (141g) All-Purpose Flour   
1 Large Egg   
½ Tsp Vanilla Extract   
Vegetable Oil, For Frying (Depends On Size Of Your Pot/Skillet) 

**Coating**   
½ Cup (100g) Granulated Sugar   
¾ Tsp Ground Cinnamon 

**Spicy Chocolate Sauce**   
8 Oz (226.8g) Chopped Semi-Sweet Chocolate   
1 Cup (238g) Heavy Cream   
2 Tbsp Unsalted Butter, Room Temperature   
⅛ \- ¼ Tsp Ground Cayenne (For Non-Spicy, Omit)   
Big Pinch Salt 

**Dulce De Leche**   
1 Cup (236g) Heavy Cream   
1 Cup (220g)Packed Dark Brown Sugar   
1/2 Cup (153g) Sweetened Condensed Milk
