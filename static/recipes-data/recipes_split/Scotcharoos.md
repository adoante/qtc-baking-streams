## **Scotcharoos**  {#scotcharoos}

**(its a chocolate peanut butter rice crispy treat \- mormons love them)** 

**SUPER EASY** 

**Difficulty:⭐️** 

1 cup sugar   
1 cup corn syrup   
1 cup peanut butter   
6 cups puffed rice cereal   
1 cup milk or semi sweet chocolate chips   
1 cup butterscotch chips 

**YOU WILL NEED:**   
Mixing bowls   
9x13 Casserole Dish  
Measuring Cups   
Pot (not weed)  
Stove Top

\*\*Do not boil the peanut butter mixture too long, if it is boiled too long it will become hard as a rock\*\*
