## **Pinwheel Sugar Cookies**  {#pinwheel-sugar-cookies}

3 Cups Flour   
½ Tsp Baking Powder   
½ Tsp Salt   
1 Cup Unsalted Butter   
1 ⅓ Cups White Sugar   
2 Eggs   
2 Tsp Vanilla Extract   
¼ Tsp Almond Extract (Super Optional)   
Red Food Coloring   
Blue Food Coloring
