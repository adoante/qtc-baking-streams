## **Strawberry Shortcake** {#strawberry-shortcake}

*Difficulty:⭐️⭐️*   
*Time: \~1 hour* 

**Equipment**   
\- Oven   
\- Mixing Bowls   
\- Baking Sheet   
\- Hand/Stand Mixer (Alt: Mix By Hand)   
\- Rolling Pin (Alternative: Wine Bottle)   
\- 3” Biscuit/Cookie Cutter [https://amzn.to/3z58jTz](https://amzn.to/3z58jTz) (Alt: a cup)   
\- Pastry brush [https://amzn.to/39WZ8tx](https://amzn.to/39WZ8tx) (optional)

**Shortcake**   
4 Cups (500g) All-Purpose Flour   
3 Tbsp Granulated Sugar   
¼ Tsp Salt   
5 Tsp Baking Powder   
¾ Cup (170g) Butter Softened   
½ Cup (113g) Butter Melted   
1 ¼ Cups (298g) Whipping Cream (Alt: Heavy Cream)   
Nonstick Spray (Pam) 

**Topping**   
2 Pints Ripe, Well-Rinsed Strawberries   
½ Cup (100g) Sugar, or more to taste   
1 ¾ Cups (416 Grams) Whipping Cream (Alt: Whipped Cream)   
¼ Tsp Vanilla Extract (Omit if you buy whipped cream)
