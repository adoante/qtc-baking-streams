## **Spudnuts** {#spudnuts}

**Ingredients-**  
2 Package Yeast  
1 ¾ Cups Warm Milk  
½ Cup Sugar  
½ Cup Mashed Potatoes  
2 Tsp Salt  
2 Eggs  
½ Cup Warm Water  
½ Cup Shortening  
1 Tsp Baking Powder  
1 Tsp Vanilla  
6 \- 7 Cup Flour

**Glaze**  
1lb Powdered Sugar  
2 Tsp Margarine  
1 Tsp Vanilla  
Hot Water

*Add enough hot water to make it thin enough to dop spudnuts in*  
*Let excess drain off.*

**Directions**  
1\. Soften yeast in warm water  
2\. Scald milk, add shortening, sugar, salt, mashed potatoes, eggs, vanilla and beat.  
3\. Add yeast and flour and make a dough too soft to knead.  
4\. Let raise double in size, roll out and cut  
5\. Let raise and cook in hot grease  
6\. Drain on paper towel and glaze
