## **Impromptu Cornbread Recipe** {#impromptu-cornbread-recipe}

Cornbread recipe was dropped when cooking dinner with fanfan 10/12/25

Makes \~2 dozen muffins

9.21 oz Flour  
9.21 oz Corn Meal  
3 oz Sugar  
½ tsp Salt  
0.4 oz Baking Powder  
0.23 oz Baking Soda  
2 eggs  
18oz Buttermilk  
1 oz Honey  
8 oz Melted Butter  
4 oz Sour Cream

350 degree F  
\~20 min bake time
