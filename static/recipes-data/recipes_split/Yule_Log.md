## **Yule Log** {#yule-log}

Difficulty:⭐️⭐️⭐️  
**BUTTERCREAM FILLING:**   
1 ⅔ cups (190g) powdered sugar   
½ cup (114g) unsalted butter, at room temperature   
2 tablespoons coffee-flavored liqueur (Alt: [https://www.fanaticallyfood.com/coffee-flavored-liqueur-substitutes/](https://www.fanaticallyfood.com/coffee-flavored-liqueur-substitutes/))   
1 ½ tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder   
1 pinch salt   
⅓ cup (57g) mascarpone cheese 

**SPONGE CAKE:**   
2 tablespoons melted butter   
½ cup (50g) unsweetened cocoa powder   
2 tablespoons all-purpose flour   
½ teaspoon kosher salt   
5 large eggs, at room temperature   
⅔ cup (150g) white sugar   
½ teaspoon vanilla   
2 tablespoons powdered sugar, or as needed 

**GANACHE FROSTING**:   
1 cup (240ml) heavy cream, boiling-hot   
8 ounce (225g) dark chocolate chips or about 2 cups

**MERINGUE MUSHROOMS:**   
1 large egg white, at room temperature   
1/16 teaspoon cream of tartar   
pinch salt   
2 Tablespoons (25g) granulated sugar   
1/2 teaspoon unsweetened natural or dutch-process cocoa powder (optional)   
1 ounces (28g) semi-sweet chocolate, finely chopped 

**YOU WILL NEED:**  
Oven   
Mixing bowls 12×17 inch or 13x18-inch pan   
Hand/Stand Mixer (Alt: mix by hand with whisk)   
Pam cooking spray (Alt: butter)  
Parchment paper   
Kitchen towel (Alt: Towel bigger than pan)   
Sifter (Optional)
