## **Viral Milk Bar Pumpkin Pie** {#viral-milk-bar-pumpkin-pie}

It has A LOT of components which make it a bit of a harder bake. Feel free to simplify if you want and get store bought pie crust and caramel (needs to be thick though) IF YOU BUY PIE CRUST AND CARAMEL it becomes a very simple bake down to a 1 star. 

Difficulty: ⭐️⭐️⭐️ (this is a harder one)   
Time: \~4 hours 

**FOR THE CARAMEL:** (Can buy this instead of making it if you want to simplify)  
1 cup granulated Sugar   
1/3 cup heavy cream   
6 tablespoon unsalted butter   
¼ cup water   
2 tablespoon light corn syrup  
2 teaspoon salt   
2 teaspoon vanilla extract

**FOR THE PIE CRUST:** (Can buy this instead of making if you want to simplify)  
1 ½ cup flour   
12 tablespoon unstalted butter   
½ cup fine ground cornmeal   
1 tablespoon granulated sugar   
1 teaspoon salt   
¼ cup water   
2 teaspoon apple cider vinegar

**PUMPKIN FILLING:**  
30 oz pumpkin puree (2 standard 15oz cans)   
1 ¼ cup (277g) heavy cream   
1/3 cup (129g) light corn syrup   
5 tablespoon (64g) unsalted butter   
2 ½ cup (443g) white chocolate chips or chopped white chocolate   
2 teaspoon cinnamon   
1 teaspoon cardamom   
½ teaspoon allspice   
½ teaspoon ginger   
1 pinch nutmeg   
1 pinch clove  
(if you can’t get the above spices, cinnamon through clove, then use 3 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice)  
½ teaspoon salt 

**CONT. BELOW**

**FOR THE STREUSSEL:**  
½ cup flour   
1/3 cup light brown sugar   
1/3 cup oats   
6 tablespoon unsalted butter   
2 teaspoon cinnamon   
½ teaspoon cardamom   
Big pinch salt 

**WHIP CREAM TOPPING**(optional) (You can buy this instead of making it to simplify) 1 cup Whipping Cream   
1/3 cup powdered sugar   
1 tsp vanilla 

**YOU WILL NEED:**   
Whisk   
Spatula   
Parchment paper   
Saucepan   
Stove top   
Oven   
Mixing Bowls   
Pie pan   
Baking sheet   
Pie weights (or you can use dry rice or beans \- or you can yolo without them your pie will just shrink a little)
