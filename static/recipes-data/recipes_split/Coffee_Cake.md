## **Coffee Cake** {#coffee-cake}

*Difficulty: ⭐️*   
*Time: \~1.5 hour* 

**Equipment**  
\- Oven   
\- Mixer (Hand/Stand)   
\- Mixing Bowls   
\- Food Processor (Alt: Fork or Pastry Cutter)   
\- 8”x 8” or 9”x 9” Square/Round Pan (9”x 9” Square Prefered)

**Cake**  
1 Stick (113g) Butter Softened   
¾ Cup (150g) Sugar   
1 Tsp (5ml) Vanilla   
1 Egg   
2 Cups (250g) Flour   
2 Tsp (10g) Baking Powder   
¾ Cup (180ml) Milk   
Pinch Salt 

**Filling**  
3 Tbsp (43g) Butter Softened   
1 Tbsp (9g) Cinnamon   
½ Cup (63g) Flour   
½ Cup (110g) Packed Brown Sugar 

**Toppings**  
5 Tbsp (70g) Butter Softened   
¾ Cup (96g) Flour   
½ Cup (110g) Packed Brown Sugar   
1 Tbsp (8g) Cinnamon
