## **Madeleines**  {#madeleines}

(its a french cake cookie \- very light and yummy) ITS EASY But it does require a certain pan do NOT attempt unless you have a madeleine pan This is the one I bought [https://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00L5HCVSG/ref=ppx\_yo\_dt\_b\_asin\_title\_o00\_s00?ie=UTF8\&psc=1](https://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00L5HCVSG/ref=ppx_yo_dt_b_asin_title_o00_s00?ie=UTF8&psc=1) 

Difficulty: ⭐️   
Estimated Time Commitment: 2.5 hours 

**Ingredients:**   
Unsalted butter: 100g (7 tbsp)   
Large eggs: 2   
Granulated sugar: 100g (1/2 cup)   
Lemon zest: 1 teaspoon (optional also can be any citrus zest you like I might do orange\!)   
Pure vanilla extract: 1 teaspoon   
All-purpose flour: 100g (3/4 cup plus 1 tbsp), sifted   
Baking powder: 1/4 teaspoon  
Salt: a pinch 

**Tools:**   
Madeleine pan (MUST)   
Mixing bowls   
Electric mixer or whisk   
Measuring cups and spoons   
Zester (for zest)   
Spatula
