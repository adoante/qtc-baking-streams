## **Lemon Poppy Scones** {#lemon-poppy-scones}

These are VERY easy \- anyone can make   
Difficulty: ⭐️ 

**Ingredients:**  
 All-purpose flour: 2 cups (240 grams)   
Granulated sugar: 1/2 cup (100 grams)   
Baking powder: 2 and 1/2 teaspoons   
Salt: 1/2 teaspoon   
Lemon zest: from 2 lemons   
Unsalted butter (frozen): 1/2 cup (113 grams)   
Heavy cream: 1/2 cup (120 ml)   
Egg: 1 large   
Vanilla extract: 1/2 teaspoon   
Poppy seeds: 1 tablespoon (9 grams)   
Lemon Juice: 2 \- 3 tablespoon   
Powdered Sugar: 1/2 cup (100 grams) 

**Tools Needed**

mixing bowl   
Box grater   
Measuring cups and spoons   
Whisk   
Baking sheet   
Parchment paper   
Citrus zester (for lemon zest)   
Citrus juicer (for lemon juice)
