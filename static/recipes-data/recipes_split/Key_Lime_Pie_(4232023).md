## **Key Lime Pie (4/23/2023)** {#key-lime-pie-(4/23/2023)}

**Difficulty:⭐️**

**FOR THE CRUST \- Oven to 375 bake for 10 min**  
12 whole graham crackers  
⅓ cup packed light brown sugar  
4 tablespoons unsalted butter (might need to add more butter if it is not sticking together like a sand castle)

**FOR THE FILLING**  
Two 14-oz cans sweetened condensed milk  
1 cup plain Greek yogurt (2% or whole milk)  
1 tablespoon grated lime zest  
¾ cup fresh lime juice

After crust lower oven to 350 and cook for 15 minutes

**FOR THE TOPPING**  
1 cup cold heavy cream  
2 tablespoons confectioners' sugar (or up to ½ cup for preferred sweetness)  
1 teaspoon grated lime zest (optional)  
½ teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)  
Lime slices (optional)

**YOU WILL NEED:**  
Oven  
Mixing bowls  
8 or 9 inch pie pan (or itty bitty pans)  
Measuring Cups   
Mixer (if you have one)
