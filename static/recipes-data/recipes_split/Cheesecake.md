## **Cheesecake** {#cheesecake}

*Difficulty Rating: ⭐️*   
*I’ll be using a 9’’x 13’’ casserole pan. You can use any kinda pan. It takes about an hour in the oven just FYI so you know for time planning. The topping is made on a stove top (for anyone planning on baking at a desk)*

**Ingredients**   
1 Package Graham Crackers   
2 Tbsp White Sugar   
1 Tbsp Brown Sugar (you can use white sugar if you don’t have this)   
7 Tbsp Butter  
32 Oz Cream Cheese (make sure this is room temp \- you can leave it out hours ahead of time. won’t be a problem even the night before is fine)   
⅔ Cup Sour Cream   
1 Cup Sugar   
1 ½ Tsp Vanilla Extract  
Salt   
4 Eggs 

**Berry Topping (Optional)**  
2 Cups Frozen Berries   
1 Lemon   
¼ Cup Sugar
