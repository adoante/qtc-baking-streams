## **Simple Chocolate Chip Cookies** {#simple-chocolate-chip-cookies}

 **Difficulty:⭐️** 

Bake-a-long streamed on 4/1/2023

1 cup butter softened   
1 cup white (granulated) sugar   
1 cup brown sugar   
2 tsp vanilla extract   
2 large eggs   
3 cups all-purpose flour   
1 tsp baking soda   
½ tsp baking powder   
1½ tsp salt   
1½ cups chocolate chips (or chunks, or chopped chocolate) \- you can also add chopped nuts if you want 

**YOU WILL NEED:**   
Oven   
Mixing bowls   
Cookie Sheet   
Measuring Cups   
Mixer (if you have one)   
Parchment paper 

Freeze Dough for 20 minutes   
Pre-heat oven to 350f/175c  
Bake 10-12 minutes
