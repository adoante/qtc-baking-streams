## **Rainbow Pride Ice Cream Cake** {#rainbow-pride-ice-cream-cake}

**Equipment**  
You need a cookie/baking sheet. This is a very thin cake and is made on a regular baking pan 11x17 Or 15x10. You will need mixing bowls, an oven, measuring cups/spoons \- this can be made without a mixer. ***must get parchment paper and gallon ziploc bags OR piping bags*** or you will be sad. Will take about 2 hours to create and it needs to freeze for a bit before consumed.

**Cake**  
1 Cup Flour   
1 Cup Powdered Sugar   
2 Tsp Baking Powder  
2 ½ Tsp Vanilla Extract   
4 Larger Eggs   
Food Coloring \- at least the primary colors so you and mix to make the rainbow   
Parchment Paper \- you WILL fail without it and gallon ziploc bags.   
1 ½ Quart Ice Cream \- any ice cream you wanna use as filling. I’m using vanilla bean
