## **Eclairs** {#eclairs}

**Equipment**  
\- Parchment Paper (You Will Need This)  
\- Pastry Bags OR Large Ziplock Bags   
\- Cookie/Baking Sheet   
\- Medium Pot  
\- Wire Cooling Rack   
\- Piping Tips (pics below of what’s required and optional)

**Shells**  
9 Tbsp (135g) Butter  
1 Cup (240ml) Water  
½ Tsp (2.5ml) Salt  
2 Tsp (10ml) Sugar  
1 Cup (120g) Flour  
4 Large Eggs, Lightly Beaten

**(Knockoff) Pastry Cream**  
1 Package Vanilla Instant Pudding 3.9 Oz (I use the Jello brand)  
1 Cup Heavy Whipping Cream (240ml)  
½ Cup Powder Sugar (120ml) 

**Glaze**  
1 ½ Cup (360ml) Semi Sweet Chocolate Chips  
1 Cup (240ml) Warm Heavy Cream

**BONUS**  
If you wanna do the little flowers on top (sometimes seen in bakeries), buy a small container of store-bought frosting and I will teach you \- we are not making our own frosting because I do not want to over complicate also food coloring if you want to make the flowers different colors
