## **Rainbow Swiss Cake Roll** {#rainbow-swiss-cake-roll}

We are doing a bake a long for a rainbow swiss roll cake (rainbow optional) THIS one is slightly more difficult only because it has a LOT of steps and tools needed but I promise any one can make it 

Difficulty: ⭐️⭐️⭐️  
Estimated Time Commitment: 2.5 hours 

**Ingredients:**  
All-purpose flour: 70 g (½ cup)   
Corn starch: 1 tbsp   
Eggs: 2 Sugar: 60 g (5 tbsp)   
Baking powder: 1 tsp   
Vanilla extract: 1 tsp   
Salt: a pinch   
Heavy cream: 200 g (¾ cup)   
Powdered sugar: 1/4 cup   
Vanilla extract: 1 tsp   
Fresh strawberries \- 1 box or any fresh berry you prefer   
FOOD COLORING \- ALL the colors of the rainbow (or whichever ones you want) 

**Tools Needed:**  
 Mixing bowls (also of small cereal bowls too for mixing your different rainbow colors   
Electric mixer or whisk  
 Measuring cups and spoons   
Spatula   
Baking sheet   
Parchment paper   
Piping Bags (or gallon zip lock bags)   
a dish clothe (clean)

**QT DO NOT USE THIS RECIPE AGAIN**   
**If you do try this recipe you need a 9x13 pan or double the recipe**
