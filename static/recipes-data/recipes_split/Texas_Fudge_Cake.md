## **Texas Fudge Cake**  {#texas-fudge-cake}

**Difficulty:⭐️**

**Ingredients:**   
1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder   
1 cup coffee   
1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter   
2 cups all-purpose flour   
2 cups granulated sugar   
1 teaspoon baking soda   
1/2 teaspoon salt   
2 large eggs, lightly beaten   
1/2 cup buttermilk   
1 teaspoon vanilla extract 

**For the Frosting:**   
1/3 cup milk   
1/4 cup cocoa   
1/2 cup butter   
1 tsp vanilla   
3 1/2 cup powdered sugar 

**You will need:**   
Offset Spatula ( i think it will help with the frosting, don't trust me tho )   
Mixing bowls   
Measuring cups and spoons   
Mixer (if you have one)   
9x13 pan
