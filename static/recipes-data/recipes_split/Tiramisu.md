## **Tiramisu** {#tiramisu}

This is a pick your journey dessert. If you want it EASY. BUY THE LADY FINGERS. They sell them at most grocery stores. IF you want a bit of a challenge you can make your own lady fingers. I will be making mine but they arent the easiest, YOU CANNOT MAKE LADY FINGERS WITHOUT A MIXER. 

**Difficulty Rating:** ⭐️ ⭐️ 

**Tiramisu Ingredients**   
Espresso or strong coffee: 1½ cups (360 ml)   
Dark rum: ¾ cup (180 ml)   
Mascarpone cheese: 16 ounces (452 g)   
Eggs: 6 large   
Granulated sugar: ⅔ cup (133 g)   
Heavy whipping cream: 2 cups (480 ml)   
Vanilla extract: 2 teaspoons   
Ladyfingers: 40   
Unsweetened cocoa powder: ⅓ cup (33 g) 

**Tools Needed**   
Mixing bowls **||** Electric mixer (hand or stand) **||** Measuring cups and spoons **||** Whisk **||** Spatula **||** Large shallow dish **||** Rectangular serving dish (around 9x13 inches) **||** Sifter or fine mesh sieve (for cocoa powder optional) 

**OPTIONAL Ladyfingers Ingredients**   
Eggs: 3 large   
Granulated sugar: ½ cup (100g)   
Vanilla extract: ½ teaspoon   
Salt: ¼ teaspoon   
All-purpose flour: 1 cup (120g)   
Cornstarch: 5 teaspoons

**Tools Needed for Ladyfingers**   
Mixing bowls **||** Electric mixer (hand or stand mixer) **||** Measuring cups and spoons **||** Whisk **||** Spatula **||** Piping bag with a round tip **||** Baking sheets **||** Parchment paper
