## **Cinnamon Rolls** {#cinnamon-rolls-1}

This recipe makes QUITE A BIT so feel free to half it if you want less. This will take a MINIMUM of 3 to 4 hours so please keep that in mind. Difficulty is higher due to time requirements and working with yeast but I promise you can do it :) 

**Difficulty:⭐️⭐️** 

**For Dough:**   
Whole Milk: 1 cup (240 ml)   
Butter: 6 tablespoons (84g)   
Yeast: 2 tablespoons (20g)   
Sugar: 1 tablespoon (12.5g)   
Water: 1 cup (240 ml)   
Eggs: 3   
Flour: 5 to 7 cups (600g to 840g)   
Sugar: 1/2 cup (100g)   
Salt: 1 teaspoon 

**For Filling:**   
Butter: 1/2 cup (112g)   
Brown Sugar: 1.5 cups (330g)   
Cinnamon: 3 tablespoons (24g) 

**For Frosting:**   
Butter (or Cream Cheese if you perfer): 1/2 cup (112g)   
Powdered Sugar: 2 to 3 cups (240g to 360g)   
Vanilla: 1 teaspoon   
Milk: 2 tablespoons (30 ml) 

**YOU WILL NEED:**   
Baking sheets   
Mixing bowls   
Electric mixer (with bread hooks) can also just knead this by hand so it is no required will just need some strength and patience   
Measuring cups and spoons   
Silicone baking mat or parchment paper   
Cooling rack
