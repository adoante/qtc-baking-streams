## **Cheesecake (9’’x13’’ Casserole Pan)** {#cheesecake-(9’’x13’’-casserole-pan)}

**Crust**  
1 Package Of Graham Crackers   
2 Tbsp White Sugar   
1 Tbsp Brown Sugar (but you can use white sugar if you don’t have this)   
7 Tbsp Butter 

**Cream Cheese**  
32 Oz Cream Cheese   
(make sure this is room temp \- you can leave it out hours ahead of time. won’t be a problem)   
⅔ Cup Sour Cream   
1 Cup Sugar   
1 ½ Tsp Vanilla Extract   
Salt   
4 Eggs

**Optional Toppings**  
Get some frozen berries, a lemon, small thing of heavy whipping cream, and powdered sugar
