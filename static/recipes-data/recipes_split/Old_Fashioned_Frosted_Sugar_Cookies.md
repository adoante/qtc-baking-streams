## **Old Fashioned Frosted Sugar Cookies** {#old-fashioned-frosted-sugar-cookies}

*Difficulty rating: ⭐️* 

**Equipment**  
Mixing Bowls, Oven, Cookie Sheet.

**Ingredients**  
1 Cup (227 G) Salted Butter, Softened  
(leave out the morning of or night before)   
¾ Cup Veggie Oil   
1 ¼ Cups (265 G) Granulated Sugar  
¾ Cup (86 G) Powdered Sugar   
½ Tsp Baking Soda  
½ Tsp Cream Of Tartar   
½ Tsp Salt   
2 Tbsp Sour Cream   
2 Large Eggs   
1 Tsp Vanilla Extract   
¼ Tsp Almond Extract   
5 ½ Cups (781 G) All-Purpose Flour   
Extra ½ Cup Sugar 

**Frosting**  
¾ Cup (170 G) Salted Butter, Softened   
2 Tbsp Sour Cream   
1 Tsp Vanilla Extract   
¼ Tsp Almond Extract   
4 Cups (684 G) Powdered Sugar   
1 \- 2 Tbsp Cream or Milk 

**Bonus**  
You can use food coloring and sprinkles if you wanna decorate
