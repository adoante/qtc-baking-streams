## **Maple Bacon Donuts** {#maple-bacon-donuts}

*Difficulty:⭐️⭐️⭐️*   
*Time: \~2.5-3 hour* 

**Equipment**  
\- Mixing Bowls   
\- Stand Mixer with Dough Hook wr Paddle Attachment   
(Alt: Mix with Wooden Spoon or Spatula)   
\- Rolling Pin (Alt: Wine Bottle)   
\- 3” \- 3.5” Doughnut Cutter [https://amzn.to/3wgivjf](https://amzn.to/3wGivjF)   
(Alt: 1 Large \+ 1 Smaller Circle Cookie Cutter [https://amzn.to/3z58jtz](https://amzn.to/3z58jTz))   
\- Parchment Paper (Alt: Silicone Baking Mats)   
\- Baking Sheets   
\- Large Heavy-Duty Pot [https://amzn.to/3lo1f02](https://amzn.to/3LO1f02)  
\- Thermometer (Not necessary; Helps to know temp of oil)   
\- Metal Spatula/Metal Slotted Spoon [https://amzn.to/3lhdtic](https://amzn.to/3lHDTic)   
\- Cooling Rack   
\- Sieve \- Option 1: [https://amzn.to/3liqv8c](https://amzn.to/3lIQv8C) Option 2: [https://amzn.to/3lkgrbt](https://amzn.to/3LKgRBT)   
\- Small Saucepan [https://amzn.to/3llml0n](https://amzn.to/3LLML0N)

**Donut**   
1 Cup (240ml) Whole Milk, Warmed To About 110°f (43°c)\*   
1 Tbsp Active Dry Yeast (Alt: Instant yeast; Rise time will be slightly shorter)   
⅓ Cup (65g) Granulated Sugar   
2 Large Eggs   
6 Tbsp (86g) Unsalted Butter, Melted And Slightly Cooled   
1 Tsp Pure Vanilla Extract   
¼ Tsp Ground Nutmeg (Use cinnamon as alternative if you don't care for nutmeg)   
½ Tsp Salt   
4 Cups (500g) All-Purpose Flour (Spoon & Leveled), Plus more as needed  
1 \- 2 Quarts Vegetable Oil (Depends on size of pot; Fill the pot ⅓ with oil)   
Nonstick Spray (Pam) 

**Toppings**  
3 Tbsp (43g) Unsalted Butter   
½ Cup (120ml) Pure Maple Syrup   
1 ¾ Cups (195g) Sifted Confectioners’ Sugar\*   
⅛ Tsp Salt   
¼ Tsp Maple Extract (Optional)   
12 Slices Cooked Bacon, Cut In Half
