## **STICKY PECAN BUNS** {#sticky-pecan-buns}

*This is a more difficult recipe cause of YEAST (we hate yeast) It also takes about*   
*3 hours so make sure to set some time aside. There is a bit of waiting like an hour and a half of just standing around bread*

**Equipment**  
\- Mixing Bowls  
\- Oven  
\- 9” X 13” Casserole Dish

**Dough**  
1 Cup (240ml) Whole Milk\*  
⅔ Cup (135g) Granulated Sugar  
1 ½ Tbsp Active Dry or Instant Yeast (2 Standard Packets)\*  
½ Cup (115g) Unsalted Butter   
(softened to room temperature and cut into 4 pieces)  
2 Large Eggs, Room Temperature  
½ Tsp Salt  
4 ½ Cups (558g) All-Purpose Flour (Spoon & Leveled) \+ More For Work Surface

**Topping**  
2 Cups (250g) Chopped Pecans  
½ Cup (1 Stick; 115g) Unsalted Butter  
⅔ Cup (135g) Packed Light Or Dark Brown Sugar  
¾ Cup (180ml) Whole Milk\*  
⅓ Cup (80ml) Pure Maple Syrup  
¼ Tsp Salt

**Filling**  
¼ Cup (60g) Unsalted Butter, Softened To Room Temperature  
½ Cup (100g) Packed Light Or Dark Brown Sugar  
2 Tsp Ground Cinnamon
