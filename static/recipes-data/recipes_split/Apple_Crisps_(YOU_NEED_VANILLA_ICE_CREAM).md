## **Apple Crisps (YOU NEED VANILLA ICE CREAM)** {#apple-crisps-(you-need-vanilla-ice-cream)}

*“Btw this is a very easy recipe ANYONE can learn this one even if you’ve never baked before including @Alan” \- QTCinderella, 2020*

**Equipment**  
\- Bowls For Mixing   
\- 9” X 13” Pan (Or Any Size Pan)  
\- 1 Pot / Stove For Making Caramel

**Ingredients**  
6 Medium Granny Smith Apples  
3 Tbsp Unsalted Butter  
1 Tbsp Lemon Juice   
3 Tbsp Water  
2 Tbsp Flour  
½ Tsp Vanilla  
⅓ Cup Brown Sugar  
1 Tsp Ground Cinnamon  
¼ Tsp Salt

\- Also \-   
½ Cup Flour   
½ Cup Old-Fashioned Oats   
½ Cup Brown Sugar   
½ Tsp Baking Soda   
¼ Tsp Ground Cinnamon  
¼ Tsp Salt   
⅓ Cup Unsalted Butter 

**Caramel Sauce (Optional)**  
½ Cup Butter   
1 Cup Light Brown Sugar   
⅓ Cup Heavy Cream
