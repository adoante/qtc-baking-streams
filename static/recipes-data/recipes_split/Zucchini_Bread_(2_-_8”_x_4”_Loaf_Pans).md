## **Zucchini Bread (2 \- 8” x 4” Loaf Pans)** {#zucchini-bread-(2---8”-x-4”-loaf-pans)}

*Or just throw it in any pan you have like who cares*

**Ingredients**  
3 Cups Flour   
1 Tsp Salt   
1 Tsp Baking Soda   
1 Tbsp Cinnamon   
½ Tsp Nutmeg   
3 Eggs   
1 Cup Vegetable Oil   
2 ¼ Cups Sugar   
3 Tsp Vanilla Extract   
2 Cups Grated Zucchini (usually 2 small/med zucchinis are enough)

**Optional:**  
1 Cup Chopped Walnuts (she don’t do this yuck nuts)   
1 Cup Turbinado Sugar OR Sanding Sugar (to sprinkle on top; she do this cause peepoFat)
