## **Pumpkin Scones with Spiced Pumpkin Glaze** {#pumpkin-scones-with-spiced-pumpkin-glaze}

*Difficulty:⭐️⭐️*   
*Time: \~1 hour* 

**Equipment**  
\- Oven   
\- Mixing bowls   
\- Baking Sheet Parchment Paper  
\- Hand/Stand Mixer (Can also mix by hand)   
\- Food processor (Can also mix by hand)   
\- 5” Biscuit/cookie cutter [https://amzn.to/3BJQoCu](https://amzn.to/3BJQoCu) (Alt: Cup)   
\- Sharp knife

**For The Scones**   
½ Cup (122 G) Canned Pumpkin Purée   
1 Large Egg   
3 Tbsp Heavy Cream   
1 Tbsp Molasses   
2 Tsp Vanilla Extract   
2 Cups (260 G) All Purpose Flour   
¼ Cup \+ 2 Tbsp (87 G) Dark Brown Sugar   
1 Tsp Baking Powder   
½ Tsp Baking Soda   
½ Tsp Salt   
1 Tsp Ground Cinnamon   
¾ Tsp Ground Ginger   
¾ Tsp Ground Cloves   
½ Tsp Ground Nutmeg   
½ Cup (113 G) Cold Unsalted Butter 

**For The Glaze**   
1 ½ Cups (174 G) Confectioners' Sugar   
3 Tbsp (46 G) Canned Pumpkin Purée   
1 Tbsp Water   
¼ Tsp Ground Cinnamon   
¼ Tsp Ground Ginger   
¼ Tsp Ground Cloves   
¼ Tsp Ground Nutmeg
