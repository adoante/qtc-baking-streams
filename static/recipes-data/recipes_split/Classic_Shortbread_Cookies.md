## **Classic Shortbread Cookies** {#classic-shortbread-cookies}

**Equipment**  
\- 1 Large Mixing Bowl  
\- 2 Or 3 Smaller Bowls  
\- Cookie Sheets For Baking,   
\- Parchment Paper OR Foil OR Cooking Spray (to prevent sticking)  
(an electric hand mixer is nice for this one but you can also just mix it by hand)

**Ingredients**  
1 ½ Cups (3 Sticks) Unsalted Butter  
(room temp \- you can leave butter out overnight or a few hours prior to baking)   
1 Cup Granulated Sugar  
1 Tsp Vanilla Extract   
3 ½ Cups All-Purpose Flour  
¼ Tsp Salt

***OPTIONAL ADDITIONS***   
Shortbread cookies are super fun to mess around with and flavor in any way you like. They are also awesome without any of these additions. Here are some optional additions I am going to add to mine. (Feel free to skip any of this) 

\- Lemon Rosemary Cookies \- 1 Whole Lemon, 2 Tbsp Rosemary 

\- Sugared Shortbread \- ¼ Cup Turbinado Sugar 

\- Cinnamon Pecan \- ¼ Cup Crushed Pecans, ½ Tsp Ground Cinnamon 

\- Chocolate Dipped \- 1 Cup Semi Sweet Chocolate OR 1 Cup White Chocolate
