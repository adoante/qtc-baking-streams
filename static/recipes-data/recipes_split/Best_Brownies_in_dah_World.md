## **Best Brownies in dah World** {#best-brownies-in-dah-world}

*Difficulty Rating: ⭐️* 

**Equipment**  
Mixing Bowls, A 8”x 8” Pan Or 9”x 13” Pan, and an Oven  
Preheat Oven To 350F / 175c

**Ingredients**  
½ Cup Butter   
(can be cold or room temperature doesn’t matter for this one)   
1 Tbsp Oil   
½ Cup Brown Sugar   
⅔ Cup Sugar   
2 Eggs   
½ Cup Flour   
½ Cup Cocoa Powder   
¼ Tsp Salt
