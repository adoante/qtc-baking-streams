## **Mochi Donuts**  {#mochi-donuts}

This requires some unique flours/starches (you can get them on amazon) and also requires frying so it is not for the faint of heart but I promise its not TOOO hard 

Difficulty: ⭐️⭐️  
 Estimated Time Commitment: 2.5 hours 

**Ingredients For the donuts:**

Mochiko flour: 150 g (about 1 cup \+ 1 tbsp)   
Tapioca starch: 115 g (about ¾ cup \+ 3 tbsp)   
Baking powder: 8 g (about 1 ¾ tsp)   
Salt: 0.8 g (½ tsp)   
White granulated sugar: 75 g (⅓ cup)   
Egg: 1 large   
Whole milk: 150 g (5 fl oz)   
Vegetable shortening: 24 g (2 tbsp)   
Vegetable oil 2 cups (for frying) 

**For the vanilla glaze:** 

Powdered sugar: 120 g (1 cup)   
Whole milk: 48 g (1 ½ fl oz)   
Vanilla extracts: ½ tsp 

**For the strawberry glaze:**   
Powdered sugar: 120 g (1 cup)   
Whole milk: 32 g (1 fl oz)   
Strawberry preserves or jam: 1 tsp (can do any flavoring in the glaze, if you want to make a chocolate one or peanut butter or matcha you can just add that to the glaze ez pz)

**Tools** 

Mixing bowls   
Whisk or electric mixer   
Measuring cups and spoons   
Deep pan (for frying)   
Parchment paper  
Piping Bag or Ziploc Bag
