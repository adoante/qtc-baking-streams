## **Brown Butter Pumpkin Oat Cookies**  {#brown-butter-pumpkin-oat-cookies}

*THIS IS STUPID EASY ANYONE CAN MAKE IT \- should only take an hour so its a quick fun one* 

**Equipment**  
All you need for this is an oven, pan, cookie sheet, and bowls

**Ingredients**  
1 Cup (2 Sticks; 230g) Unsalted Butter   
2 Cups (170g) Old-Fashioned Whole Rolled Oats   
1 ⅔ Cup (208g) All-Purpose Flour   
1 Tsp Baking Soda   
½ Tsp Salt   
1 ½ Tsp Ground Cinnamon   
1 ½ Tsp Pumpkin Pie Spice   
1 Cup (200g) Granulated Sugar   
¾ Cup (150g) Packed Light Or Dark Brown Sugar   
1 Egg Yolk, Room Temperature   
2 Tsp Pure Vanilla Extract   
1 Cup (228g) Pumpkin Puree

*I like to top this with maple glaze but it is totally optional* 

**Maple Glaze**   
2 Tbsp (30g) Unsalted Butter   
⅓ Cup (80ml) Pure Maple Syrup   
1 Cup (112g) Powdered Sugar   
Pinch Salt, To Taste
