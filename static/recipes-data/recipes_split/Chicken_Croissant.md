## **Chicken Croissant** {#chicken-croissant}

**Ingredients:**  
Cooked Chicken  
Stick of Cream Cheese  
2 tbsp Lemon Pepper  
1 tbsp Garlic Powder  
Seasoning (Salt, Pepper etc to taste)  
8oz Chopped Can Mushrooms  
Croissant Dough (preferrably ready made)  
Coating (Melted Butter, Breadcrumbs, Almond Powder)  
Gravy 

**Directions:**

1. Preheat the oven to 350 Fahrenheit.   
2. Shred the cooked Chicken.  
3. Mix in the cream cheese, Can of Mushrooms, Lemon Pepper, Garlic Powder and additional seasonings to taste.  
4. Get Croissant Dough and roll out to like ½ inch.  
5. Cut out circles of the dough and place Chicken mixture in the middle.  
6. Pull up the dough over the mixture in the middle and pinch as you would with dumplings.  
7. Roll this “dumpling” in melted butter, then the breadcrumbs and the almond powder.  
8. Place in the oven for 15 minutes.  
9. Once cooked, serve with gravy.
