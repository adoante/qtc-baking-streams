## **Crack**  {#crack}

(it’s a really good snack / bar / cookie like thing that’s addictive so people call it crack) 

**Difficulty:** ⭐️

**Ingredients**  
1 box graham crackers   
1 cup butter   
1 cup brown sugar   
1 cup pecans (optional also can do any nut you like)   
2 cups chocolate chips   
1/4 cup white chocolate chips (optional for decoration) 

**MUST HAVE**   
foil and a cookie sheet

**Will also need**   
mixing bowls, oven, pot, stove top, measuring cups, and mixing spoons.
