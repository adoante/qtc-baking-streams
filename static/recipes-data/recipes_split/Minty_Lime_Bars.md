## **Minty Lime Bars**  {#minty-lime-bars}

*Difficulty: ⭐️⭐️* 

**Equipment**  
\- 8”x 8” or 9”x 9” Square/Round Pan (If you use a traditional 9”x 13” rectangle casserole dish you will want to double the recipe)  
\- Aluminium Foil   
\- Bowls, Oven, a Pot, and a Whisk.   
\- Stove Top  
(No mixer at all required)

**Shortbread Crust**  
2 Tsp Butter or Pan Spray \- to grease the pan  
2 Tbsp Finely Grated Lime Zest (from about 3 limes)   
¼ Cup (50g) Granulated Sugar   
1 Cup (130g) All-Purpose Flour   
2 Tbsp (Packed) Finely Chopped Fresh Mint   
¼ Tsp Baking Powder   
½ Cup (113g) Cold Unsalted Butter  
Salt

**Lime Curd**  
¾ Cup (170g) Fresh Lime Juice (from about 7 limes)   
¼ Cup (57g) Fresh Lemon Juice (from about 1 large lemon)   
1 Tsp Cornstarch   
1 Cup (200g) Granulated Sugar   
5 Large Egg Yolks   
1 Large Egg   
6 Tbsp (85g) Cold Unsalted Butter  
Confectioners’ Sugar and Finely Grated Lime Zest (Optional) \- for topping  
Salt
