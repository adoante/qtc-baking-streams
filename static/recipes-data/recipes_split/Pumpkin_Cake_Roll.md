## **Pumpkin Cake Roll** {#pumpkin-cake-roll}

*Should take about 1 hour to make \- 2 if I get distracted* 

**Equipment**  
You MUST have a cookie sheet for this and will need parchment paper or a dish towel, as always mixing bowls and a hand mixer will help make the frosting easier but it is possible without

**Ingredients**  
¾ Cup (95g) All-Purpose Flour  
1 Tsp Baking Powder  
½ Tsp Baking Soda  
1 ½ Tsp Ground Cinnamon  
1 Tsp Pumpkin Pie Spice  
¼ Tsp Salt  
3 Large Eggs, At Room Temperature  
⅔ Cup (150g) Canned Pumpkin Puree  
½ Cup (100g) Packed Light Or Dark Brown Sugar  
½ Cup (100g) Granulated Sugar  
1 Tsp Pure Vanilla Extract  
For Rolling: ¾ Cup (90g) Powder Sugar  
(you will need parchment paper or a clean dish towel for this)

**Cream Cheese Frosting**  
6 Oz (170g) Block Cream Cheese, Softened To Room Temperature  
¼ Cup (½ Stick; 60g) Unsalted Butter, Softened To Room Temperature  
1 ¾ Cups (210g) Powder Sugar  
1 Tsp Pure Vanilla Extract
