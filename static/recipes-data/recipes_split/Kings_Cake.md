## **Kings Cake** {#kings-cake}

*Difficulty: ⭐️⭐️⭐️*   
*\*\*This is a bread more than a cake so it will take about 4 hours\*\**

**Bread**  
½ Cup Milk   
2 Tbsp Butter   
1 (.25 Oz) Package Active Dry Yeast   
¼ Cup \+ 1 Tbsp \+ 1 Tsp Warm Water   
¼ Cup White Sugar   
1 Egg   
1 Tsp Salt   
½ Tsp Nutmeg   
2 ¾ Cups All-Purpose Flour 

**Filling**  
¾ Cup (165g) Light Brown Sugar Firmly Packed  
2 Tsp Ground Cinnamon   
4 Tbsp (57g) Unsalted Butter Melted 

**Icing**  
1¼ Cup (150g) Powdered Sugar  
2 Tbsp Milk   
½ Tsp Vanilla Extract   
Green, Purple and Yellow Sanding Sugar
