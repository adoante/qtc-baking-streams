## **Scones**  {#scones}

*Each recipe makes \~8 scones*

**Equipment**  
\- Baking Sheet  
\- Mixing Bowls  
\- Cheese Grater  
\- Parchment Paper (Optional)

**Pumpkin Scones**  
2 Cups (250g) All-Purpose Flour (Spoon & Leveled)  
2 ½ Tsp Baking Powder  
1 Tsp Ground Cinnamon  
1 ½ Tsp Pumpkin Pie Spice  
½ Tsp Salt  
½ Cup (115g) Unsalted Butter Frozen   
(yes throw your butter in the freezer the night before)   
⅓ Cup \+ 2 Tbsp (105ml) Heavy Cream, Divided  
1 Large Egg  
½ Cup (115g) Canned Pumpkin Puree  
½ Cup (100g) Light Brown Sugar  
1 Tsp Pure Vanilla Extract  
Optional: Coarse Sugar (for sprinkling on top before baking)

**Maple Glaze**  
2 Tbsp (30g) Unsalted Butter  
⅓ Cup (80ml) Pure Maple Syrup  
1 Cup (112g) Sifted Confectioners’ Sugar  
Pinch Salt, To Taste

**Caramel Apple Scones**  
2 Cups (240g) All-Purpose Flour (Spoon & Leveled), Plus More For Hands And Work Surface  
2 ½ Tsp Baking Powder  
1 ¼ Tsp Ground Cinnamon  
½ Tsp Salt  
½ Cup (115g) Unsalted Butter Frozen  
(yes throw your butter in the freezer the night before)   
½ Cup (120ml) Heavy Cream (+2 Tbsp for brushing)  
1 Large Egg  
½ Cup (100g) Packed Light Or Dark Brown Sugar  
1 Tsp Pure Vanilla Extract  
1 Heaping Cup (125g) Peeled And Chopped Apple (I use Granny Smith)  
Optional For Topping: Coarse Sugar

**Caramel Sauce**  
1 Cup (200g) Granulated Sugar  
6 Tbsp (90g) Salted Butter (room temperature cut up into 6 pieces)  
½ Cup (120ml) Heavy Cream  
1 Tsp Salt
