## **Soft Peanut Butter Cookies**  {#soft-peanut-butter-cookies}

**Difficulty Rating: ⭐️** 

**Ingredients:**   
1 cup creamy peanut butter ( 240g)   
1 cup packed brown sugar ( 200g)   
1 cup white sugar (200g)   
1 cup butter or 1 cup margarine, softened ( 227g for butter ) or ( 250g for margarine )   
2 eggs   
1 teaspoon baking soda (6g)   
1 teaspoon baking powder ( 4.79g)   
1 teaspoon vanilla extract ( 4,2g)   
2 1⁄2 cups flour (312.5g) 

**YOU WILL NEED:**  
Mixing Bowls   
Mixer   
Whisk   
Spatula   
Baking sheets   
Parchment Paper or Baking Mat   
Fork   
Measuring Cups   
Cooling Rack
