## **Banana Bread (9” Loaf Pan / 9” Round / 8” x 8” / 9” x 13”)** {#banana-bread-(9”-loaf-pan-/-9”-round-/-8”-x-8”-/-9”-x-13”)}

**Ingredients**  
½ Cup Butter   
1 Cup Sugar   
2 \- 3 Ripe Bananas   
2 Eggs   
1 Tsp Vanilla Extract   
2 Cups Flour   
1 Tsp Baking Soda   
½ Tsp Salt   
½ Cup Sour Cream 

**“Artificially ripened bananas”**  
Warm unpeeled bananas on a baking sheet in a 250° oven for 15-20 minutes. It's not quite as effective as letting bananas ripen naturally, but it will soften and sweeten your fruit in a pinch.
