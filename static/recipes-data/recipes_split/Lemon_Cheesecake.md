## **Lemon Cheesecake** {#lemon-cheesecake}

Difficulty Rating: ⭐️ \- hardest part is the curd but that is optional 

**For the crust:**   
Graham Cracker Crumbs: 2 1/4 cups (270g)   
Brown Sugar: 3 tablespoons (37.5g)   
Butter: 7 tablespoons (98g) 

**For the filling:**   
Cream Cheese (room temperature): 32 oz (908g)   
Sour Cream: 2/3 cup (164.15g)   
Sugar: 1 cup (200g)   
Vanilla Extract: 1.5 teaspoons   
Lemon Juice: 3 teaspoons   
Lemon Zest: 3 tablespoons   
Salt: a pinch   
Eggs: 4 

**For the lemon curd (optional):**   
Granulated Sugar: 1 cup (200g)   
Lemon Zest: 2 tablespoons (zest of two lemons)   
Egg Yolks: 6   
Lemon Juice: 1/2 cup (120ml)   
Butter (unsalted, cold): 1/2 cup (113.5g)   
You'll need a total of like 6 lemons for this

I’ll be using a 9’’x13’’ casserole pan. You can use kind of any pan. BUT IT WILL OVERFLOW IF YOU USE ONE SMALLER THAN 9x13 half the recipe. It takes about an hour in the oven just FYI so you know for time planning. 

**YOU WILL NEED:**  
Mixing bowls   
Measuring cups and spoons   
Electric mixer   
Rubber spatula   
Zester   
Juicer   
Oven   
Small saucepan   
Whisk   
Fine mesh strainer (optional)
