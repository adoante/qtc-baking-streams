## **Cream Puffs** {#cream-puffs}

**Difficulty:** ⭐️⭐️⭐️ (this is a harder one)  
Time: \~3 hours 

**Ingredients for Choux Pastry:**

1/2 cup water (118 ml)  
1/2 cup whole milk (118 ml)  
8 Tbsp unsalted butter (113 grams)  
1 tsp granulated sugar (4 grams)  
1/4 tsp salt (1.5 grams)  
1 cup all-purpose flour, measured correctly (120 grams)  
4 eggs, (large), room temperature

**Ingredients for Cream Filling and Garnish:**

2 cups heavy whipping cream, chilled (473 ml)  
1/2 cup powdered sugar  
1 tsp vanilla extract (5 ml)  
28 raspberries, optional

**YOU WILL NEED:**  
Measuring Cups and Spoons  
Mixing Bowls  
Saucepan  
Wooden Spoon or Spatula  
Baking Sheet  
Parchment Paper or Silicone Baking Mat  
Piping Bag with a Round Nozzle (or just a gallon zip lock bag) x2  
Electric Mixer or Whisk (optional)  
Oven
