## **Pie Master Classes (5 pie options)** {#pie-master-classes-(5-pie-options)}

**Chocolate Silk Pie**  
½ Cup Butter, Room Temp (113g)  
¾ Cup White Sugar (150g)  
2 (1 Ounce) Squares Unsweetened Baking Chocolate, Melted And Cooled  
1 Tsp Vanilla Extract

**Crust**  
Store Bought Oreo Crust Or  
24 Oreos (2 Cups (268g) Oreo Crumbs)  
4tbsp (56g) Butter, Melted (Salted Or Unsalted Is Fine)

**Banana Cream Pie**  
1 Box (5.1 Oz) Jell-O Banana Cream Instant Pudding Mix  
1 Cup Cold Milk (240 Ml)  
½ Cup Whipping Cream (125 Ml) (Alt: Cool Whip)  
½ Tsp Vanilla Extract  
¼ Cup Powdered Sugar (25g)  
2 Bananas

**Crust**  
Store Bought Graham Cracker Crust Or  
1 ½ Cups Finely Ground Graham Cracker Crumbs (180g)  
⅓ Cup Brown Sugar (65g)  
6 Tbsp Unsalted Butter, Melted  
½ Tsp Ground Cinnamon (Optional)

**Pecan Pie**  
3 Eggs  
½ Cup Corn Syrup (350g)  
½ Cup Honey (170g)  
1 Cup Brown Sugar (213g)  
2 Tbsp Butter (Melted)  
1 Tsp Vanilla Extract  
⅛ Tsp Salt  
1 ½ Cups Pecans (190g)

**Crust**  
Store Bought Pie Crust Or  
1 ¼ Cup All-Purpose Flour (150g)  
¼ Tsp Salt  
10 Tbsp Unsalted Butter (Cold And Cut Into Cubes)  
2-4 Tbsp Ice Water (As Needed)  
Pie Weights (Beans, Pennies, Marbles Etc)

**Pumpkin Pie**  
15oz Pumpkin Puree (425g)  
3 Eggs  
½ Cup Brown Sugar (100g)  
2 Tsp Cinnamon\*  
½ Tsp Salt  
½ Tsp Nutmeg\*  
½ Tsp Cloves\*  
½ Tsp Ginger\*  
½ Tsp Pepper  
½ Cup Milk (120ml)  
½ Cup Heavy Cream (170g)  
\*Cinnamon, Nutmeg, Cloves And Ginger Can Be Substituted With 3 Tsp Pumpkin Pie Spice

**Crust**  
Store Bought Pie Crust Or  
1 ¼ Cup All-Purpose Flour (150g)  
¼ Tsp Salt  
10 Tbsp Unsalted Butter (Cold And Cut Into Cubes)  
2-4 Tbsp Ice Water (As Needed)  
Pie Weights (Beans, Pennies, Marbles Etc)

**Caramel Apple Pie**  
½ Cup Salted Caramel  
8 Large Apples, Cored, Peeled And Sliced Into ¼ Inch Slices (10 Cups For \~1.25kg Total)  
\*Qt Used Green Apples But Any Firm Apples Will Work\*  
½ Cup Granulated Sugar (100g)  
1 Tbsp Fresh Lemon Juice (15ml)  
¼ Cup All-Purpose Flour (31g)  
¼ Tsp Ground Allspice Or Ground Cloves (Either Is Fine)  
¼ Tsp Ground Nutmeg  
1 ½ Tsp Ground Cinnamon  
1 Large Egg  
1 Tbsp Milk (15ml)  
Coarse Sugar For Sprinkling On Crust (Optional)

**Homemade Salted Caramel**  
1 Cup Granulated Sugar (200g)  
6 Tbsp Salted Butter, Room Temperature Cut Up Into 6 Pieces (90g)  
½ Cup Heavy Cream, Room Temperature (120ml)  
1 Tsp Salt

**Crust**  
Store Bought Pie Crust Or  
1 ¼ Cup All-Purpose Flour (150g)  
¼ Tsp Salt  
10 Tbsp Unsalted Butter (Cold And Cut Into Cubes)  
2-4 Tbsp Ice Water (As Needed)  
Pie Weights (Beans, Pennies, Marbles Etc)
