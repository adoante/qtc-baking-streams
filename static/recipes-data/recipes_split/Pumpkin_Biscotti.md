## **Pumpkin Biscotti** {#pumpkin-biscotti}

*Biscotti is like a crunchy Italian biscuit/cookie*  
*They are great to dip into your coffee or tea*

**Equipment**  
All you need for this is a few mixing bowls, oven, and a cookie sheet

**Ingredients**  
4 Tbsp (57g) Butter, Softened  
⅔ Cup (131g) Granulated Sugar  
1 ¼ Tsp Pumpkin Pie Spice  
(OR ¾ Tsp Cinnamon \+ ¼ Tsp Each Nutmeg And Ginger)  
¾ Tsp Cinnamon  
1 ½ Tsp Baking Powder  
½ Tsp Salt   
1 Large Egg  
½ Cup (113g) Pumpkin Purée  
2 Cups (241g)  All-Purpose Flour  
Optional: Coarse Sparkling Sugar or Cinnamon Sugar
