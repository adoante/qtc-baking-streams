## **Lemon Cookies** {#lemon-cookies}

EZ PZ EVERYONE CAN MAKE THESE 

**Difficulty:⭐️** 

**Ingredients:**  
Unsalted butter: 1 cup (226g)   
Sugar: 1 cup (200g)  
Egg: 1 large   
Lemon zest: From 3 lemons   
Lemon juice: 5 tbsp (45ml)   
Vanilla extract: 1 tsp (5ml)   
All-purpose flour: 2 1/2 cups (312g)   
Baking powder: 1 tsp (5g)   
Salt: 1/4 tsp (1.5g)  
Powdered sugar: 1 cup (120g) 

**YOU WILL NEED:**   
Baking sheets   
Mixing bowls   
Electric mixer (optional)   
Measuring cups and spoons   
Whisk  
Zester (for lemon zest)   
Silicone baking mat or parchment paper   
Cooling rack   
Small bowl (for mixing the glaze)   
Spoon or whisk (for drizzling the glaze)
