## **Oatmeal M\&M Cookies** {#oatmeal-m&m-cookies}

1 Cup Unsalted Butter   
1 Cup Brown Sugar   
¼ Cup White Sugar   
2 Eggs   
1 Tbsp Vanilla Extract   
1 Tbsp Molasses ( if you can’t find this it’s fine just leave it out)   
1 ½ Cups Flour   
1 Tsp Baking Soda   
1 ½ Tsp Cinnamon   
½ Tsp Salt   
3 Cups Old-Fashioned Whole Rolled Oats   
1 ½ Cups M\&Ms (they have special red white and blue ones for 4th of July,   
I encourage this if you wanna be festive![:Jammies:][image3])
