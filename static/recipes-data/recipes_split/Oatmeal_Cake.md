## **Oatmeal Cake** {#oatmeal-cake}

*Difficulty:⭐️⭐️* 

**Equipment**   
\- Oven   
\- Mixing Bowls   
\- 9”x 13” Or 9”x 9” Pan  
\- Hand/Stand Mixer (Alt: Mix By Hand)   
\- Pam Cooking Spray (Alt: Butter)

**Oatmeal Cake**:   
1 ½ Cups (315ml) Boiling Water   
1 Cup (80g) Rolled Oats   
½ Cup (113g) Unsalted Butter   
1 Cup (220g) Brown Sugar   
1 Cup (200g) Granulated Sugar   
2 Eggs   
1 ½ Cups (188g) All-Purpose Flour   
1 Tsp Baking Soda   
¾ Tsp Salt   
1 ½ Tsps Cinnamon   
1 Tsp Vanilla Extract 

**Oatmeal Cake Icing:**   
½ Cup (113g) Unsalted Butter   
1 Cup (220g) Brown Sugar   
¾ Cup (180g) Heavy Cream   
1 ⅓ Cup (200g) Pecans (Chopped)   
2 Cups (190g) Shredded Coconut   
¼ Tsp Vanilla Extract   
½ Tsp Salt
