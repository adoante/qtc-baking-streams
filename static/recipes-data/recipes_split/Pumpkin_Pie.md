## **Pumpkin Pie** {#pumpkin-pie}

**Tools**  
mixing bowls, aluminum foil, a pie tin, a working oven \- everything else we can improvise \- if you have a mixer your life will be easier

**Pie Dough**  
½ Cup Unsalted Butter   
1 ¼ Cups All-Purpose Flour  
½ Tsp Salt  
Water

**Filling**   
1 15-Oz Can (About 2 Cups; 450g) Pumpkin Puree  
(DO NOT BUY PUMPKIN PIE FILLING \- Get Pumpkin Puree)  
3 Large Eggs  
1 ¼ Cups Light Or Dark Brown Sugar  
1 Tbsp Cornstarch  
½ Tsp Salt  
1 ½ Tsp Ground Cinnamon  
½ Tsp Ground Ginger  
¼ Tsp Ground Nutmeg  
⅛ Tsp Ground Cloves  
⅛ TspFresh Ground Black Pepper  
1 Cup Heavy Cream  
¼ Cup Milk (Any %)

**Extra**  
1 Extra Egg  
2 Cups Heavy Cream  
½ Cup Powdered Sugar   
½ Tsp Vanilla Extra  
A bag of rice or beans to use as pie weights
