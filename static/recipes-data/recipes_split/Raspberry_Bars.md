## **Raspberry Bars** {#raspberry-bars}

Difficulty:⭐️   
THIS IS A QT RECIPE \- and it is super duper easy NO MIXER NEEDED \- ANYONE CAN MAKE THIS

Bake-a-long streamed on 3/25/2023

Ingredients:  
2 1/4 Cups Oats   
2 1/4 Cups Flour   
1 1/2 Cup Brown Sugar   
2 Tsp Baking Powder   
1 1/3 cup COLD butter   
1 1/2 Raspberry Preserves (can be any preserve, strawberry, orange marmalade etc)  
1 tsp salt 

YOU WILL NEED:   
Oven   
Mixing bowls   
9x9 inch or 9x13-inch pan   
Parchment paper

Oven Temp: 325f/160c

Cook time: \~45 minutes, top should be golden brown and the jam will be bubbling on the sides.
