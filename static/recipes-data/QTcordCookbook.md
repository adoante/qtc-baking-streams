## **Ninja’s Cheesecake** {#ninja’s-cheesecake}

*Difficulty rating: ⭐️*  
*I highly suggest using a springform pan for this. It makes it much easier to remove from the pan. Amazon link at bottom.*  
*\*\*\*You will also need a large enough pan to fit the entire cake pan into for the water bath. Without a water bath the cake can take on a rubbery texture. The water controls how much heat enters the cake.\*\*\**

**Crust**  
1 ½ Cups Graham Crackers Crumbs  
¼ Cup Sugar   
5 Tbsp Melted Butter

**Cheesecake**  
4 8-Oz Blocks of Cream Cheese **\*ROOM TEMP\***  
8 Oz Sour Cream **\*ROOM TEMP\***  
1 Cup Sugar  
1 Tsp Vanilla Extract   
2 Tsp Fresh Lemon Juice  
3 Large Eggs **\*ROOM TEMP\***

**Set oven to 350°F (177°C)**

**Make the crust**  
Use a blender or food processor to grind graham crackers into crumbs. If you don’t have these then you can use the back of a spoon to crush, but you’ll be there for a while. Once ground into fine crumbs, add sugar and crumbs into a bowl and mix together. Then melt your butter. Careful not to let it pop in the microwave, you need all of it. Stir butter into the mixture and break up any large chunks. It will be sandy/grainy. Pour into a 9 inch springform pan or any other 9 inch circular pan. Lightly pat down until there’s no more loose crumbs. Use a measuring cup to even it out if needed. Pre-bake the crust for 10 minutes. Set aside to cool while you make the filling.

**Make the cheesecake filling**  
Using a stand mixer or large bowl, add sugar and cream cheese into the bowl and beat until smooth & creamy. Then add sour cream, lemon juice, and vanilla then mix until fully combined. Next, add each egg one at a time, waiting until the previous has been fully incorporated. Once the last egg is fully incorporated, stop mixing. This helps the cake not deflate and crack when it cools.  **\***Wait to pour into the pan till you have prepared a water bath.**\***

**Preparing the water bath & baking**  
Any pan large enough to fit the cake pan will do. If you are using a springform pan you will need to cover the outside with aluminum foil to prevent water from getting in. 2 layers will be enough.   
Place the crust in the pan and fill with 1 inch very hot/boiling water. Then pour in the filling and smooth out with a rubber spatula.  
Bake for 55-70 minutes, center should be almost set. If it is browning too quickly, tent it with aluminum foil during baking. Cake is done when the center slightly shakes when you move the pan. Turn off the heat and leave in the oven for 1 hour. Then take it out of the oven and water bath to cool uncovered to room temperature. Then cover and chill overnight or for at least 4 hours.

Coating (Melted Butter, Breadcrumbs, Almond Powder)  
Gravy 

**Directions:**

1. Preheat the oven to 350 Fahrenheit.   
2. Shred the cooked Chicken.  
3. Mix in the cream cheese, Can of Mushrooms, Lemon Pepper, Garlic Powder and additional seasonings to taste.  
4. Get Croissant Dough and roll out to like ½ inch.  
5. Cut out circles of the dough and place Chicken mixture in the middle.

8. Place in the oven for 15 minutes.  
9. Once cooked, serve with gravy.

## **Whipped Cream Cake** {#whipped-cream-cake}

**Ingredients:**  
1 cup butter, softened  
3 cups sugar  
6 eggs  
3 cups cake flour, sifted  
1 cup heavy whipping cream  
1 teaspoon vanilla extract  
1 teaspoon lemon extract  
Fresh berries or fruit to garnish  
Confectioners sugar

**Recipe (16 servings):**  
Heat oven to 325 degrees.  
Cream the butter and sugar in a mixing bowl until light and fluffy.  
Add the eggs, flour and whipping cream and mix well.  
Stir in flavourings.  
Spoon into a greased and floured bundt pan.  
Bake for 1½ hours or until it tests done.  
Cool in pan for several minutes.  
Invert onto wire rack and cool completely.  
Garnish with confectioners sugar and fresh berries.
